Pokémon Wiki

On this site you will find all information you may need need about each pokémon, you can navigate the website using the navigation bar found near the top of the web page. Below is a quick overview of what is available.

This site is meant as an information hub/wiki for all 898 Pokémon that currently exist in the Pokémon universe. You will find detailed pages for each Pokémon as well as pages for their alternate forms (Mega, Gigantamax, etc.) if they have one.

If you register an account and login you will be able to save Pokémon to your favourites, you can access these favourites by clicking on Saved or your username in the navigation bar.

On the Search page you will be able to search for specific Pokémon based on how much of the form you fill out, you can fill out none of the form and met wth a table containing all Pokémon or you be more specific and fill out any number of the fields to narrow the search.

The Regions page allows you to search pokemon based on region and offers a quicker alternative to the search form.

You can also find individual pages for each Pokémon by clicking on their names or sprites when they viewed from a table. You will be taken to their Pokémon Dex page and from there can go back and forth between pages. On these Pokédex pages you will find out all sorts of information on that specific Pokémon.

Also available is a Random Team Generator, here you will be able to generate random teams of Pokémon. Each team will six Pokémon all with a unique primary type, by default the team will be generated from all 898 Pokémon but this can be narrowed down by region by using the buttons available at the top of the page.
